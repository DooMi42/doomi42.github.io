Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

This portfolio website is designed to be privacy-friendly. We collect minimal information:

  • Contact Form Data: When you use the contact form, we collect your name, email, and message content
  • Analytics Data: Basic website usage statistics (if analytics are enabled)
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and device information for technical purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Responding to your contact form submissions
  • Improving website functionality and user experience
  • Technical maintenance and security purposes

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Under GDPR, we process your personal data based on:

  • Legitimate Interest: Responding to business inquiries and maintaining website functionality
  • Consent: When you voluntarily submit contact form information

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Data is stored securely and encrypted where possible
  • Access to personal data is limited to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary:

  • Contact Form Data: Up to 2 years after last contact
  • Analytics Data: Up to 26 months (if applicable)
  • Data is automatically deleted after the retention period

6. Your Rights Under GDPR

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests

7. Third-Party Services

This website may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts: For typography (privacy-friendly implementation)
  • Hosting Provider: For website hosting and technical services

These services may have their own privacy policies and data processing practices.

8. Cookies

This website uses minimal cookies for essential functionality:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website operation
  • Analytics Cookies: Optional, for website improvement (if enabled)

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

9. International Data Transfers

If data is transferred outside the EU/EEA, we ensure adequate protection through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Adequacy decisions by the European Commission
  • Other appropriate safeguards as required by GDPR

10. Children's Privacy

This website is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new privacy policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Sending you an email notification (if applicable)
